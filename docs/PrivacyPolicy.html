Nine Windows - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Overview

Nine Windows is a golf practice training app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is stored.

Data Collection

Nine Windows does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no servers, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

Data Storage

All data created within the app — including practice session results, session notes, practice goals, driving range configurations, and your preferences — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData and UserDefaults frameworks. This data never leaves your device.

Data Export

Nine Windows includes an optional CSV export feature that lets you share your session history. This export is initiated entirely by you and uses the standard iOS share sheet. The app does not automatically send data anywhere — you choose where the file goes.

Third-Party Services

Nine Windows does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms. The app makes no network requests.

Tracking

Nine Windows does not track you across apps or websites. The app does not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track.

Children's Privacy

Nine Windows does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the support email provided on the App Store listing.
